how to compile

1   mkdir bin

2   javac -d bin -cp "lib/mysql-connector-j-9.3.0.jar" ^
      src/com/naufal/hideandseek/Main.java ^
      src/com/naufal/hideandseek/model/*.java ^
      src/com/naufal/hideandseek/view/*.java ^
      src/com/naufal/hideandseek/presenter/*.java

3   xcopy src\assets bin\assets /E /I /Y

4   java -cp "bin;lib/mysql-connector-j-9.5.0.jar" com.naufal.hideandseek.Main
